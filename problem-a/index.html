Barry Allen

Barry Allen is from the tv show The Flash. In the show he is the fasest man alive. When he was a child he saw his mother killed and saw his father falsely accused for her murder. Then lightening struck him and gave him super speed. To most people, he is an ordinary forensice scientist, but secretly he uses his speed to fight crime.

Reasons to like the show:

  • Character development is well developed in the show
  • A lot of life lessons can be taught by watching this show
  • Comicbook fans will enjoy the easter eggs that they put into the show
